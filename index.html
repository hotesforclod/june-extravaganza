Squad Norse: Rockefeller
Sunday, June 21st · Morning
The Mission
A Midtown takeover with a Norse twist — waffles, toys, roller skating, and World Cup gear. Back by 2 PM for Mario's Times Square run.
🧇 Stop 1 — Scandinavia House
58 Park Ave. Norwegian waffles to kick off the Norse mission. Open from 11 AM.
🧸 Stop 2 — FAO Schwarz
30 Rockefeller Plaza. The legendary toy store — floor piano, Build-a-Bear, Barbie boutique, larger-than-life everything.
⛸️ Stop 3 — Roller Skating at The Rink
Rockefeller Plaza. The rink goes roller skating every summer — under Prometheus with Midtown all around you.
⚽ Stop 4 — FIFA World Cup Store
51st St at Rockefeller Center. The squad picks their gear — jerseys, scarves, collectibles.
